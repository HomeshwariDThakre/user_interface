The Walk

The Taj Mahal has three entry gates: South，East, and West. The West Gate is the most crowded entrance mainly used by locals and tour groups, and the South Gate doesn't open until 8 am. If you want to watch the sunrise, it is recommended to stay at a hotel near the East Gate which is nearby many good hotels.

What?

The Taj Mahal has three entry gates: South，East, and West..

Where?

The Taj Mahal is located on the right bank of the Yamuna River.

Price?

The Walk is free!
